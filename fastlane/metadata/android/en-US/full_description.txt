<h1 id="gesture-drawing-practice">Gesture Drawing Practice</h1>
<h2 id="vpt-003-gdp">VPT#003-GDP</h2>
<p>This is Gesture Drawing Practice, an open source app for artists. It displays your selected pictures from the gallery, each for a set amount of time (like 1 minute). There are options to display a countdown timer and duration of pause (blank screen) between pictures</p>

<h2 id="features">Features</h2>
<ul>
<li>🤖 select multiple pictures, order is preserved (!)</li>
<li>⏲️ choose from 4 different usual time options for practice</li>
<li>😴 choose from 4 different useful pause options </li>
<li>🎨 options for visibility and appearance of the count down timer </li>
</ul>
<h2 id="usage">Usage</h2>
<ul>
<li>Tap the big red button</li>
<li>Select your pictures from the gallery</li>
<li>Change settings or leave it as it is</li>
<li>Tap &quot;Start Session&quot </li>
</ul>
